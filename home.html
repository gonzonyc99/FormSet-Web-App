Welcome to FormSet's Online Wall Rendering Tool!

To get started please enter the Height and Width of your wall! Once you have entered this information you can choose from several pallets with designs to drag and drop and create your custom wall!
If your wall will be used on a stage, please enter the height and width as well!
“Welcome to the Design Page! Choose from one of FormSet’s designs to the left, drag and drop the square where you’d like it on the grid, and add color to each block using the color picker.

Preview what your wall will look like in your venue with your stage by selecting “Preview” and uploading a picture of your venue.

Once you designed your wall, add a video screen, a silhouette for frame, or even your logo as a final touch!

When you’re ready, click on the export tab to download your rendering!”
